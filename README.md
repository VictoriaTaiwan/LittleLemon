This is a mobile food ordering app. 

Registration Page will welcome new customer and ask to create an account.

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/faae7de0-6585-4639-9a0e-50cf955a3d17" width="300" height="700" />

***

Registered user can browse Little Lemon Menu and choose a meal. 
User can find a meal by name or category. 
Cart button will lead to the customer's shopping list.

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b370e7ba-adf1-4cb1-aebb-38939a74b798" width="300" height="700" />

***

Once meal card is clicked customer can add it to their cart, go to the Cart or Home Page.

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b9dcf98b-8d28-45ba-a064-172d38a2424b" width="300" height="700" />

***

Inside Cart user can remove meal from their list or continue shopping in Home Page.

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e8c66162-147f-448e-8a20-d0b32fda2dbf" width="300" height="700" />
